LA TALE IS LIVE ON PAPAYA PLAY

After weeks of anticipation, La Tale has officially relaunched through Papaya Play! The time has come to return to the magical lands of Jiendia and Freios, and adventure with Heroes old and new. Keep reading for more information on the relaunch festivities and community events, plus details on the latest massive update to LaTale, Call of the Goddess!

Papaya Relaunch Rewards

To celebrate La Tale’s arrival in its new home, the La Tale Team is giving out special rewards to both returning Legacy Heroes and to those completely new to the game:

  • Returning players who applied for a Legacy Account will receive their exclusive Legacy Tier Rewards in their inventory when logging in for the first time as a huge thank-you for prior years of service. If you had an existing La Tale account through OGPlanet and haven’t yet completed the Transfer Process, you can still do so for a limited time.
  • Upon logging in for the first time new players will receive a special Papaya Package**, which includes useful items that every Hero needs to start their new adventure right!

**All Legacy Accounts will also receive the Papaya Package.

Call of the Goddess

Releasing alongside the Papaya Play relaunch, Call of the Goddess is the next thrilling chapter of the La Tale story.Available for Heroes Level 200 and above, venture into the Temple of Seres, the besieged home of the Demigods, and beyond, on the trail of an ancient demonic Monarch and the missing Goddess Seres. In addition to the story expansion, Call of the Goddess also features new Legend Skills, over 100 new quests, 5 new zones, 30+ new monsters and more! Check out the Call of the Goddess update page on the La Tale Website for more details.

Community Events

The La Tale Team is running several exciting Community Events through the relaunch period, meaning there are plenty of opportunities to earn some great prizes. Login everyday to participate in the Attendance Events, and then show off your skills in the Challenge Events for a chance to win exclusive prizes like Permanent Titles and Custom Guild Emblems. There will also be a number of Leveling Events running through the relaunch period to help Heroes reach to the Call of the Goddess content, including the new Runestone system.

Check out the La Tale News Feed for full details on all current and upcoming Community Events.

There’s never been a better time to return to the magical world of La Tale! Stay tuned to the La Tale Facebook Page, Twitter Account and Official Forums for all La Tale updates and news.
